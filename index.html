Marvel Vs. DC in the Theater.

Based on The Verge article: Marvel's movie business is crushing DC's and it's not close.

The Verge is my most frequented website for all things gadget and culture related. When they came out with an interesting data driven article about the performance in the theaters of Marvel's movie adaptations compared to DC's I knew I needed to visualize it.

The embedded spreadsheet they included in the article was awesome but made it hard to understand the themes in the data. I hope that this visualization will help make them more apparent.

I hope you enjoy and of course if you have any questions/comments or sage life advice tweet at me @NicholasStrayer.

How to use

  • Click the different menu options in the upper right corner to filter the data by type:
    • "Total (adjusted)" is the worldwide gross in dollars adjusted for inflation.
    • "Total" is the worldwide gross not adjusted for inflation.
    • "Domestic" gross in the United States.
    • "Foreign" gross outside of the United States.
